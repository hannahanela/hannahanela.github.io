About Me

I'm a software engineering student based in Oakland, California.

As a student of the Rithm School May 2022 cohort, I'm excited to bring my passion for collaboration and creative placemaking to the tech field. A cellist for 25 years, I specialized in experimental classical music and noise. In 2017 I co-founded the Omaha based nonprofit OutrSpaces. A performing arts hackerspace by day and concert venue by night, OutrSpaces gave artists resources to hone their craft, network with other performers, and connect with their audience.

I'm eager to explore how tech can bring people together in ways that feel approachable and thoughtful, with an element of the unexpected.
