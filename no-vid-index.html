Jobly

React | Node | Express | PostgreSQL

Jobly is a job listing single page React application with authentication and search functionality. A user registers or logs in and receives a personalized login confirmation message and private routes accessible only through authentication. Users can browse all job listings and companies, as well as search by company name or job title.

Warbler

Flask | PostgreSQL

Warbler is a Twitter clone Flask app with user authorization and authentication. Once registered or logged in, a user sees a feed of 100 most recent "warbles" from only users they follow or their own. Users can post warbles, follow/unfollow other users, like warbles, and search for users. Profile editing features data validation and CSRF protection.

About Me

Hi, I'm Hannah!

I'm a full stack software engineer based in Oakland, California.

A graduate of the Rithm School, I'm excited to bring my passion for collaboration and creative placemaking to the tech field. I was a cellist for 25 years specializing in experimental classical music and noise. In 2017 I co-founded the Omaha-based nonprofit OutrSpaces. A performing arts hackerspace by day and concert venue by night, OutrSpaces gave artists resources to hone their craft, network with other performers, and connect with their audience

I'm eager to explore how tech can bring people together in ways that feel approachable and thoughtful, with an element of the unexpected.
